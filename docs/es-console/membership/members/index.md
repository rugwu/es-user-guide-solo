# Member Details

After you click into a member record, you go the Member details page, which gives you a wide variety of options for managing the member details, the account, and their card(s).

![An overview of the landing page with the Insights button](../../../../images/Member%20Details%20Page%20with%20Issuances%20Tab.png)


The header section outlines the following information about the member:

- **Card Number or External Identifier**: Valid card number including the Business Identification Number (BIN) or an External Identifier used by the client to identify the member.

- **Member First Name/Last Name**: This is the first and last name of the member as entered at the time of registration

- **Employee Status (only some implementations)**: If member is an employee, the status says “Employee”, if not it will say “Non-Employee.” **Note:** Whether this feature is shown depends on your Console configuration.

- **Account Status**: Displays the current status of the account (various statuses are available).

- **Card Status**: Displays the current status of the card (various statuses are available).

- **Registration**: How the member registered (e.g., through an app = Mobile) and the registration date.

- **Last Activity Date**: The date of the last activity as something that affected the reward balance.

- **Account Balance**: All points earned on the account; note that points just added may not be reflected here because of a configurable time-based hold on those added points. If the member is in a household, the balances will be titled to reflect that and the balances will be household balances rather than the individual balances for this member.

- **Available Balance**: Points balance minus the configurable time-based hold (placed on all points just added as a fraud prevention measure). For instance, points may not be redeemable for 12 hours after they were earned (the length of the hold is provided in the interface).

- **Redeemable Balance**: The total points a member can currently redeem

- **Redeemable Value**: The currency value of the redeemable points (for example, displayed in dollars)


There are also three processes that can be managed from the section on the right-hand side of the header:

- **Post-Purchase Earn**: Allows points to be awarded on a transaction that could not be awarded when the transaction occurred (for instance, due to the member forgetting to bring their card to the store)
- **Discretionary Credits**: Allows points to be added to or removed from the member's account at the discretion of the Customer Service Agent (for instance, if the amount rewarded on a transaction is incorrect)
- **Notes**: Allows the Customer Service Agent to make notes about the member, account, cards, and other details of interactions for future reference.

On the body of the page, there are multiple tabs and expanding options that you can use to manage the members:

- **Account, Cards, Balance Tab**: Includes options to manage the member's profile, cards, and status; there are several expandable sections under this tab.
- **Promotions Tab**: Allows viewing of the member's interaction with Targeted Offers and Banners; there are three tabs under this tab.
- **Transactions Tab** Allows viewing of the member's transactions in the system.
- **Issuances Tab**: Provides access to the record of issuances (such as vouchers) to the member.
