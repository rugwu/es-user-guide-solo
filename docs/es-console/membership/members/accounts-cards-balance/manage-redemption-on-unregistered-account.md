# Manage Redemption on Unregistered Account


An account is unregistered if the email address for the member has not yet been provided. If an account has not been registered, the **Manage Account Status** section looks different than it will for a registered account and the account status will be set to **Unregistered**.

For some clients, an agent in the Console needs a means to allow a redemption on a ghost (unregistered) card, specifically for someone with a card that wants the benefits of being in the loyalty program but without providing Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as their email address, date of birth, or phone number. For these members, the agent must set the ability to redeem points earned on the unregistered card.

 ![An overview of an unregistered account](../../../../../images/Allow%20Ghost%20Card%20Redemption.png)

### To allow redemption for an unregistered member

1. Click **Membership > Members**.
2. Select **Card Number**, enter the number of the unregistered card, and click **Search**.
3. Click on the record returned to open it in the **Member** landing page.
4. Scroll down to the section that is titled **This account is Unregistered** and expand that section.
5. Under **Redemption Override**, select the checkbox next to **Allow redemption for this unregistered member**.
6. Click **Update Account**. Redemption is now allowed on this account.
