## Transfer Points

The **Transfer Points** section allows an agent, on behalf of a member, to transfer points from the member's account to another account (the recipient account). They may want to do this to share accumulated points with a family member or friend or as a gift. Points can only be transferred from one account to another account, two accounts at a time. There is currently no feature that allows point transfers between multiple pairs of accounts or from one to many or many to one in one operation.

:::info
This section will not appear if configuration is not set up to use Transfer Points or if the user does not have the correct permissions. The source account must be **Active** (not Suspended) to transfer points. In addition, the **External Identifier** option may not be available for point transfers, depending on configuration.
:::

### To Transfer Points:

1. From the top menu of the Console, select **Membership > Members**. Select any option and fill in a valid value, then click **Search**. Then click on the record returned to open the member details page. The **Member** page of the source account opens.
2. Click in the **Transfer Points** section to expand that section.
3. Select whether to search for the recipient by a **Card Number**, the member's **Email address**, their **Phone Number**, or another account identifier (subject to configuration). If you select **Partner Link ID** (if available), you will select a partner name in the dropdown that appears when you select it.
4. Enter a value in the field next to the **Search** button.
5. Click **Search**. The agent search, including the parameters used, is included in an audit trail so that searches may be reviewed. As long as there is a corresponding account, information for the recipient account is shown in the section.
6. Click the **Transfer** button (the green button with a star and two arrows).

    ![An overview of the points transfer process](../../../../../images/Multiple%20Options%20-%20Email%20Selected.png)

7. In the **Transfer Points** window, the **Available** balance of points that may be transferred is shown. Enter the number of points to transfer. Review the details of the points transfer, then click **Proceed**.

    ![An overview of the points transfer process](../../../../../images/Multiple%20Options%20-%20Provide%20Points%20and%20Confirm%20Transfer.png)

8. In the **Apply Points Transfer** confirmation window, click **Accept** to complete the points transfer.

    ![An overview of the points transfer confirmation window](../../../../../images/Multiple%20Options%20-%20Apply%20Points%20Transfer.png)

You will see a **Points were transferred successfully** message. In addition, the relevant points balances at the top of the page will be adjusted immediately by the number of points that you transferred. The balance for the recipient account will be immediately credited with the points transferred. There is no hold on transferred points, so they can be used right away.

### To view Points Transfer transactions in the sender or recipient account:

1. From the top menu of the Console, select **Membership > Members**. Select any option and fill in a valid value for the source or destination account for a points transfer, then click **Search**. Then click on the record returned to open the member details page. The **Member** page of the source account opens.
2. Below the headers, click the **Transactions** tab. You will see the **Points Transfer** transactions (this is listed in the **Transaction Type** column) both for inbound and outbound transferred points.
3. To view additional details of the transaction, click the **Details** button.
