# Other Member Information

:::info
To view the **Other Member Information** section, your company must be using this feature, and you as an agent must have a Role which includes the VIEW_MEMBER permission. 
:::

This section includes three tabs to show different types of member data:

1. **Extended Member Data**: Additional data relevant to the member used by the client for their purposes or by Exchange Solutions to provide additional member information.
2. **Member Scoring & Intelligence**: Shows member inclusion in any scores developed through the MSI feature.
3. **Member Aggregations**: Shows totals for a selected aggregation such as SPEND - YEAR.

## Extended Member Data Tab

The Extended Member Data (EMD) tab accesses additional data that is relevant to the member and is used by your company for various purposes, but which is not central or required for ES Loyalty™ to work. EMD can be used for additional processing in your systems or for reporting. The Extended Member Data tab is shown in view-only mode.

The Extended Member Data (EMD) tab accesses additional data that is relevant to the member and is used by your company for various purposes, but which is not central or required for ES Loyalty™ to work. EMD can be used for additional processing in your systems or for reporting. The Extended Member Data tab is shown in view-only mode.

### Working with Extended Member Data

1. From the top menu, select **Membership > Members**. Select a search option, enter a value, and click **Search**. Click on one of the member records returned.
2. On the **Member** landing page, expand the **Other Member Data** section on the page. The **Extended Member Data** tab is selected as it is the default.
3. To view additional pages, click one of the other pages numbers that are not highlighted at the bottom of the page (where Previous and Next are shown).
4. In the **Show** dropdown, select a different number of items to show on the current page to view more results.
    ![An overview of the extended member data tab](../../../../../images/Other%20Member%20Data%20-%20EMD%20Tab.png)

:::tip
Note that extended member data will accommodate "null" or blank values.
:::

## Member Scoring & Intelligence Tab

The **Member Scoring & Intelligence** tab provides information about the scores generated from MSI in which the current member is included. The **Member Scoring & Intelligence** tab is shown in view-only mode.

### Working with Member Scoring & Intelligence data

1. From the top menu, select **Membership > Members**. Select a search option, enter a value, and click **Search**. Click on one of the member records returned.
2. On the **Member** landing page, expand the **Other Member Information** section on the page. Click the **Member Scoring & Intelligence** tab to select it.
3. To view additional pages of Member Scoring & Intelligence data, click one of the other pages numbers that are not highlighted at the bottom of the page (where **Previous** and **Next** are shown).
4. In the **Show** dropdown, select a different number of items to show on the current page to view more results.
    ![An overview of the member scoring and intelligence tab](../../../../../images/Other%20Member%20Information%20-%20Member%20Scoring%20and%20Intelligence%20Tab.png)


:::note
The NAME of the score is 128 characters by default, but this is configurable.
:::

## Member Aggregations Tab

The Member Aggregations tab provides information about aggregation values for a selected member.

### Working with Member Aggregations data

1. From the top menu, select **Membership > Members**. Select a search option, enter a value, and click **Search**. Click on one of the member records returned.
2. On the **Member** landing page, expand the **Other Member Data** section on the page. Click the **Member Aggregations** tab to select it.
3. Select an aggregation from the **Aggregation Name** dropdown list to display the relevant data. The aggregations shown are configurable to client requirements, but might include selections such as:
    1. **Spend - Year**: Spending by the selected member for this calendar year.
    2. **Spend - Month**: Spending by the selected member for this calendar month.
    3. **Rewards - Month**: Rewards earned by the selected member for this calendar month.
    4. **Redeem - Month**: Redemptions by the selected member for this calendar month.

    For any selected aggregation, a message is shown if there is no data available for that aggregation. There may be multiple entries for an aggregation, based on the aggregation selected and the number of periods available for that member. Depending on the length of the member's transaction history, there may be quite a few entries.

4. To view additional pages of Member Aggregations data, click one of the other pages numbers that are not highlighted at the bottom of the page (where **Previous** and **Next** are shown).
5. In the **Show** dropdown, select a different number of items to show on the current page to view more results.
    ![An overview of the member aggregations tab](../../../../../images/Member%20Aggregations%20with%20Multiple%20Lines.png)
