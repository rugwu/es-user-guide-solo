# Transfer Ghost Card Balance

A ghost card is a card that has not yet been associated with the required information for member registration (the member's email address). This card can accumulate points by being associated with a transaction, but cannot be used to redeem points until registration takes place (unless an agent allows redemptions on the ghost card; see **Manage Redemption on Unregistered Card** for more information). However, the points can be transferred to an active account, whether held by the same member or not.

:::info
This feature may not be available depending on product configuration and permissions.
:::

### To transfer the balance from a ghost card to this account

1. Select **Membership > Members**. Select a search method, enter a value, and click **Search**. Then click on one of the member records returned. The member details page opens.
2. Click on the **Transfer Ghost Card Balance** section to expand it.

    ![An overview of the transfer ghost card balance section](../../../../../images/Transfer%20Ghost%20Card%20Balance.png)

3. Enter the ghost card number. If you want to make the ghost card the active card, make sure that **Make this the active card** is selected. Otherwise, see the Notes below.
4. Click **Transfer Balance**. The reward points from the ghost card are transferred to the current account.
5. In the confirmation window, click **Transfer Balance and Update** to confirm the transfer of points. The ghost card is set to **Cancelled**.

    ![An overview of the transfer ghost card balance cancel section](../../../../../images/Transfer%20Ghost%20Card%20Balance%20and%20Cancel%20Card.png)


:::info
- If **Make this the active card** is *not* selected, the current active primary card receives the points from the ghost card specified, but the ghost card is then cancelled. This change is summarized in the confirmation window (step 5 above).
- Points transferred from the ghost card to the active card are added to the point balance. The transactions are also moved from the ghost card to the **Transactions** tab for the active card. However, transferring points from a ghost card in this manner cannot change the member's tier status.
:::

---

### To make the ghost card the active card

1. Select **Membership > Members**. Select a search method, enter a value, and click **Search**. Then click on one of the member records returned. The member details page opens.
2. Click on the **Transfer Ghost Card Balance** section to expand it.
3. Enter the ghost card number.
4. Select **Make this the active card**.
5. In the confirmation window, click **Transfer Balance and Update**. The points from the current account will be transferred to the ghost card number entered and it becomes the **Active** card and the previous active card is set to **Cancelled**.

:::info
As **Make this the active card** is selected, then the ghost card becomes the active primary card and the current active primary card is cancelled. This change is summarized in the confirmation window (step 5 above).
:::
