# Member Account Links

Member account links are links between a loyalty program member's account and specific payment cards (financial service partner), loyalty cards (retail partners), or business units. For instance, a member's account may be linked to payment cards from other companies that are partnered with the client's company including debit or credit cards. 

Or the account may be linked to loyalty cards used by a retailer or another business unit. Through this linking, the member may have access to additional benefits such as rewards for enabling the link, additional base rewards and offers, and access to an expanded product catalog.

Information about the member account links is shown in an expandable section on any specific member's information page. Agents cannot manage links from the Console. Linking is done by members selecting a partner and facilitated through the relevant programmatic calls.

:::info
This section will appear for members who are linked to different partners and/or business units. If the member is not linked, this section will not appear. Note that this page is read-only.
:::

## To view the member account links:

1. From the top menu of the Console, select **Membership > Members**. Select a search method and enter the information for that method. Click **Search**, then click on the record returned to open the member details page.

2. Click in the **Member Account Links** section to expand that section. You will see links associated with that account. View the field descriptions below.

The following fields are displayed for members linked to payment cards:

- **Partner** - The name of the partner who provides the payment card.

- **Date Linked** - The date that the link to this payment card was made.

- **Card Type** - The type of card, "Debit" or "Credit."

- **Card Number** - The last four digits of the card number.

These fields are displayed for members linked to a business unit:

- **Unit** - The name of the linked Business Unit.

- **Date** - The date that the link to this Business Unit was made.

- **Status** - The status of this link, "LINKED" if active.

- **Link ID** - The unique identifier for this link.
