# Find Members

:::note
The features available in your version of Console depend on which features are enabled. Make sure that directions you follow below are the correct directions for your version (compare the screen capture image to your version to make sure). 
:::

An agent or marketer can search for a member in the Console by using **Membership > Members**, then entering a value, selecting a search type, and clicking **Search**. This search by a user or agent, including the parameters used, is included in an audit trail so that the searches may be reviewed.

Depending on the configuration for your company, you will be able to search for members using one or more of the following criteria:

- Card number or email address
- Phone number
- Account ID or Business Name
- Account, family association, or **Bill To / Ship** name
- Partner Link ID 

![How to search for members](../../../../images/Members%20-%20Search%20for%20Members.png)

## Finding a Member by Card Number or Email

Search for a member from the top navigation menu by selecting **Membership > Members**. You can find Members by searching on one of the criteria. 

- **Card Number**: Search on the member’s unique card number. Fill in the entire card number and click **Search**.
- **Email**: Search on the member’s unique email address in standard email format. Enter the email address, then click **Search**.

After you click **Search**, the information for all matching members is shown in the table on the page. Click anywhere in the field of a specific member record to open the details page for that member. To clear the search information returned, click **Clear Search**.

## Finding a Member by Phone Number

Search for a member from the top navigation menu by selecting **Membership > Members**. You can find **Members** by a phone number by searching on a number. 

:::note
The display of phone number search depends on your company's requirements and may be different in your Console.
:::

Select the **Phone Number** selector, enter the phone number in the search window, then click **Search**. Note that as you begin to enter the number, the number is displayed in the correct format: (aaa) nnn-mmmm. If you search for an incomplete phone number, no results will be returned.


![Searching for members with a phone number](../../../../images/Members%20-%20Search%20Phone%20Number%20on%20Entry.png)

After you click **Search**, the information for all matching members is shown in the table on the page. Click anywhere in the field of a specific member record to open the details page for that member. 

To clear the search information returned, click Clear Search.

## Find a Member by Account ID or Business Name

Once they have been created, you can search for a member from the top navigation menu by selecting **Membership > Members**.

:::note
The display of member account IDs or business names depends on your company's requirements and may be different in your Console.
:::

You can **Find a Member** by searching on one of the criteria:

- **Account ID**: Search on the member's unique Account Identification number. Note that this feature is only available to some clients. To use it, enter the **Account ID** and click **Search**.
- **Business Name**: Search on the name of the business associated with the member. This will return all members associated with that **Business Name**. Note that this feature is only available to some clients. To use it, enter the **Business Name**, then click **Search**.

After you click **Search**, the information for all matching members is shown in the table on the page. Click anywhere in the field of a specific member record to open the details page for that member. 

To clear the search information, click **Clear Search**.



## Find a Member by Account or Family Association or Bill To/Ship To Affiliation


Search for a member from the top navigation menu by selecting **Membership > Members**. You can find **Members** by Account Association by searching on one of the related criteria. See the information above for searching on Card Number or Email.

:::note
The display of account associations depends on your company's requirements and may be different in your Console.
:::

- **Bill To/Ship To**: Search on the member’s account association in the form of a "Bill To" or "Ship To" account. A Bill To account is an account that handles payment for one or more Ship To accounts. Fill in the exact association name, then click **Search**.
- **Family Circle/Account Association**: Search on the member’s family associations (as in a family of companies or stores) or account associations. Fill in the exact Family Circle name, then click **Search**.

After you click **Search**, the information for all matching members is shown in the table on the page. Click anywhere in the field of a specific member record to open the details page for that member. 

To clear the search information returned, click **Clear Search**.


## Find a Member by Partner Linking ID

Search for a member from the top navigation menu by selecting **Membership > Members**. You can find Members by a partner linking ID by searching on one of the related criteria. 

:::note
The display of partner linking IDs depends on your company's requirements and may be different in your Console.
:::

- Partner Linking ID: In the dropdown menu next to **Partner Linking ID**, select the relevant partner from the selections in the dropdown list. Enter the partner link ID in the search field, then click **Search**.

After you click **Search**, the information for all matching members is shown in the table on the page. Click anywhere in the field of a specific member record to open the details page for that member. 

To clear the search information returned, click **Clear Search**.
