The **Issuances** tab provides a history of issuances such as vouchers associated with the member and includes options to control the number of issuances displayed and the page of results. The Issuances tab shows the same vouchers information that the loyalty member can see in their self-serve offer gallery.

:::note
This feature may not be available in your version of Console depending on configuration.
:::

### List of Issuances

The main part of the tab page shows a list of issuances that involved the member. 

**Heading information:**

- **Issued Date** timestamp - The date/time when the issuance occurred.
- **Expiration Date** timestamp - When the issuance expiry is scheduled.
- **Type** - The type of issuance (for example, "ISSUANCE" or "VOUCHER").
- **Status** - The status of the issuance (for instance "AVAILABLE" for use, "REDEEMED" or already used, or "EXPIRED" or not used before the **Expiration Date**).
- **Code** - A unique identifier (letters, numbers, and underscores) to identify the issuance.
- **Amount** - The amount of the issuance to two decimal points if in dollars or in points.
- **Notes** - Any additional explanatory notes about the issuance.


### Controlling the Number of Issuances Shown and Additional Pages of Results

By default, a maximum of 20 issuances are shown in the list, but this can changed using the Show control. The current page can also be selected.

**To control the number of issuances shown in the list:**

1. Select **Membership > Member**, then enter a card number or other identifier to find the member. Click on their record in the results to navigate to the Member details page.
2. Click the **Issuances** tab.
3. Use the controls at the bottom of the page to set the number of issuances to **Show** on the page (between 20 and 100) and additional pages of results (**Previous/Next** or click a page number).
