:::note
Membership features cannot be used in ES Promo Enhance™.
:::

The member management features are accessed from the **Membership** menu. By selecting **Members**, conducting a member search, and clicking into a member record, you can access the member management features in Console on the member page. The Membership menu also allows access to Audiences, or collections of members used for specific purposes. In addition, you can access the Member scoring and Intelligence features from this menu to manage Gen AI-generated members scores according to your criteria.

- Members // linked to find a member
- Audiences // linked to audiences
- Member Scores // linked to member scoring and intelligence

Members are created through a registration process. They can be found and managed through the Console. The Console allows management of a member's profile, account, cards (including ghost cards), promotions, and transactions. In addition, a service agent can manage any post-purchase earns or discretionary credits and make notes related to that member and their account.

:::note
If the account is closed, several features on the member page are disabled:

- Buttons are disabled for **Post Purchase Earn** and **Discretionary Credits** (on the page header toolbar), the **Load to Card** button () for unloaded offers under the **Promotions** tab, **Invite Member** (in **Households**), and **More** (in **Account Status**).
- The **Update Profile** button in the **Member Profile** section. The fields are also greyed out to indicate that they cannot be edited, and if **Anonymize Member Data** was selected when closing the account, some fields will show no or masked content.
:::
